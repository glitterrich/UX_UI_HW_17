About Me

As a board-certified Behavior Analyst, my background is in applied behavior analysis. I worked on a caseload of really unique clients, conducting observations, evaluations, and stakeholder interviews. The findings of these conversations were used to create tailored treatment and support plans for children and teenagers with disabilities, which were then carried out by my trained staff and family members. I bring my expertise in the scientific principles of behavior to my work in UX Research. Behavioral observation, data collection, interviewing, user testing, training staff, team management, and data analysis are at the forefront of my skills. I also have a keen eye for UI Design and style. I am passionate about inclusive, user-centered design. My dream is to play a part in making technology more accessible for all humans.
